## 🌱 Current private projects

### 🌿 CERES — Legal RAG Chatbot

CERES is a Legal RAG chatbot designed to answer questions using retrieved source material rather than relying only on the model's memory.

It is designed to work with uploaded legal documents, UK government sources, legislation, case law, and matter-specific files. The goal is for each answer to show or describe the material it relied on.

**Current stack:** FastAPI · Next.js · retrieval pipeline · Docker  
**Status:** Private while authentication, access control, audit logging, and data-security controls are still being developed.

### 🛡️ VESTA — Legal Chatbot Evaluation Framework

VESTA is a standalone evaluation framework for legal chatbots and LLM tools.

It runs beside a chatbot as a separate repository, sends test questions, receives answers, scores them against expected legal criteria, and writes diagnostic reports.

VESTA is designed to evaluate the full legal-chatbot pipeline, including final answer quality, legal factuality, citation grounding, RAG quality, jurisdiction fit, stale-law risk, abstention safety, citation validation, and agent/tool workflow traces.

**Status:** Private while the framework is still being developed.
